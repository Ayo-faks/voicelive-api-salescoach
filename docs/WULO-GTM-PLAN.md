# Wulo — UK Go-To-Market Plan

> **Product:** Wulo — therapist-supervised AI speech practice for children with SEN
> **Domain:** sen.wulo.ai
> **Market:** United Kingdom
> **Timeframe:** 6 months from launch

---

## 1. Market Context

| Signal | Data |
|--------|------|
| Children struggling with speech & language in the UK | ~2 million (Speech and Language UK) |
| Children on NHS SALT waiting lists (Nov 2024) | 65,114 |
| Waiting >12 weeks | 45.6% of the above |
| SLT vacancy rate across UK | 21% (RCSLT, 2023) |
| NHS community health waiting lists | >1 million pathways (NHS England, Feb 2024 onward) |
| RCSLT/NHS Elect transformation toolkit | Commissioned 2025, due April 2026 |
| Primary teachers reporting post-COVID language delays | 67% (Speech and Language UK, 2023) |

**Bottom line:** Demand for paediatric SALT vastly outstrips supply. The system is actively looking for scalable, supervised practice tools that complement — not replace — clinical sessions.

---

## 2. Positioning

### One-liner
> Wulo helps speech therapists extend care beyond the session — giving children guided, AI-powered practice, parents something concrete to do at home, and therapists structured review without adding hours.

### Positioning statement
For speech and language therapists, schools, and families waiting for or receiving SALT services, Wulo is a therapist-supervised practice platform that delivers guided, child-friendly voice exercises with real-time pronunciation feedback and structured session review. Unlike consumer speech apps (Speech Blubs, articulation drill apps) or virtual therapy marketplaces (Mable, BetterSpeech), Wulo sits inside the therapist's workflow — not alongside it — so practice is clinically directed, progress is measurable, and the therapist stays in control.

### What Wulo is NOT
- Not a diagnostic or assessment tool
- Not a replacement for a qualified SLT
- Not an unsupervised consumer app for children
- Not a teletherapy marketplace

## 4. Messaging by Audience

### For Therapists
**Headline:** "Your practice buddy between sessions."
**Body:** Wulo gives your clients guided, child-friendly speech exercises that follow your targets. You set the sounds, words, and difficulty. The child practises with a calm AI buddy. You get structured review — articulation, engagement, pronunciation — without listening to 30-minute recordings.
**CTA:** "Start a free pilot with 5 children."

### For Schools / SENCOs
**Headline:** "Structured speech practice while children wait for therapy."
**Body:** 65,000+ children are waiting for SALT. Wulo gives your pupils supervised practice they can do in school or at home, aligned to their targets, with progress reports for you and the therapist. No additional SLT hours required.
**CTA:** "Book a 20-minute SENCO demo."

### For Parents
**Headline:** "Practice that feels like play."
**Body:** Wulo is a warm, guided speech practice app your child uses with a friendly buddy. Their therapist sets the exercises. You see what they practised and how they did. It's not screen time — it's structured, supervised practice.
**CTA:** "Ask your therapist about Wulo."

### For Local Authorities / NHS
**Headline:** "Reduce waiting-list drift with supervised practice at scale."
**Body:** Wulo provides structured, non-diagnostic speech practice for children on SALT waiting lists. Therapist-directed, GDPR-compliant, safeguarding-first. Measurable outcomes for commissioning teams. Integrates into existing pathways without replacing clinical provision.
**CTA:** "Request a pilot proposal."

## 6. Pricing

### Therapist Plan
| Tier | Price | Includes |
|------|-------|----------|
| Starter | Free | 3 children, 2 built-in exercises, basic review |
| Pro | £49/month per therapist | Unlimited children, custom exercises, full review, parent sharing |
| Clinic | £39/month per therapist (3+ seats) | Everything in Pro + clinic-level dashboard |

### School Plan
| Tier | Price | Includes |
|------|-------|----------|
| Pilot | £1,500/term (up to 30 children) | Full platform, SENCO dashboard, termly report |
| Full | £4,000/year (up to 60 children) | Everything in Pilot + parent access, SLT integration |

### Local Authority / NHS
| Tier | Price | Includes |
|------|-------|----------|
| Waitlist pilot | £10,000–£25,000/year per cohort | Defined cohort, outcome reporting, governance pack |
| Borough-wide | Custom | Multi-school, multi-cohort, data integration |

> **Pricing philosophy:** Start low enough that a single therapist can say yes without a procurement process. Scale pricing to institution size. Never charge per child for therapists — it creates perverse incentives against onboarding more children.

## 9. Risks and Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Therapists see Wulo as replacing them | Position explicitly as practice support, not therapy. Therapist controls all exercises. Never market as "AI speech therapy." |
| Regulatory concern (children's data, AI, health) | GDPR compliance, no health claims, safeguarding-first design, DPIA ready |
| Low parent adherence | Design for < 5 min sessions, gamification, therapist nudges, parent progress view |
| NHS/LA procurement drag | Start with therapists and schools (no procurement). Build governance pack in parallel for Month 5+ |
| Consumer app competitors (Speech Blubs etc.) undercut on price | Don't compete on consumer. Win on therapist trust and clinical workflow integration |
| Mable Therapy expands into AI practice | Move fast on therapist adoption. Mable is a services business — Wulo is a platform. Different unit economics. |
