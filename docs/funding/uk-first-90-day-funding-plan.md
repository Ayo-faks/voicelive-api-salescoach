- Treat Innovate UK as opportunistic rather than central unless a highly aligned competition opens.

## Funding story Wulo should use across applications

Use this framing consistently:

Wulo is a therapist-supervised pediatric speech practice platform that helps children with speech-language needs receive more structured, engaging, and measurable practice between live therapy sessions. It improves continuity of care, supports therapist review, and helps families and clinicians turn recommendations into actual monitored follow-through.

Lead benefits to emphasize:

1. improved access to structured practice outside scarce clinician time;
2. better continuity between sessions;
3. stronger visibility for therapists and caregivers;
4. more efficient use of specialist workforce time;
5. relevance for underserved children with SEN and different learning needs.

Avoid leading with:

1. Azure architecture;
2. generic AI claims;
3. consumer language app framing;
4. any suggestion that Wulo replaces clinical judgment.

## Core metrics to gather during this 90-day window

These will improve every grant or accelerator application:

1. number of therapist users or pilot sites;
2. number of child sessions completed;
3. repeat session rate;
4. completion rate for home or supervised exercises;
5. therapist-reported time saved in review or planning;
6. parent or caregiver follow-through rate;
7. qualitative feedback from therapists on workflow and safety;
8. any early signal on pronunciation or engagement improvement.
