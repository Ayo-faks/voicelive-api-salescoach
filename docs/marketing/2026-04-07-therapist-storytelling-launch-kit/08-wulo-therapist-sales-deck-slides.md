# Wulo Therapist Sales Deck

**Format:** Presentation markdown  
**Audience:** Independent paediatric SLTs and small clinic owners  
**Goal:** Convert interest into a pilot conversation or therapist walkthrough

# Wulo

## Keep care working between sessions

So your care keeps working, even when the session ends.

Therapist-supervised speech practice for children.

---

# The Problem

## The session is not the whole therapy story

- Progress depends on what happens between appointments
- Home practice is often inconsistent or unclear
- Parents want to help but need structure
- Therapists spend too much time catching up instead of moving forward

Speaker note:
The biggest care gap is often not inside the appointment. It is what happens after the child leaves and before they come back.

---

# What Breaks Down

## Good targets often turn into weak follow-through

1. Therapist sets the target
2. Family tries to support practice
3. Child practices inconsistently
4. Therapist has limited visibility before the next session

Speaker note:
Even when the clinical target is right, the between-session loop is often fragile.

---

# The Opportunity

## Children need more continuity, not more confusion

- One hour a week is rarely enough on its own
- Families need something concrete to do
- Therapists need leverage without giving up control
- Better continuity can improve what happens in the next session

---

# The Wulo Answer

## Wulo is the practice layer between sessions

- Therapist sets the plan
- Child gets guided speech practice
- Parent gets a clear supporting role
- Therapist reviews structured progress before the next appointment

Speaker note:
Wulo is not trying to become the therapist. It supports therapist-led care between appointments.

---

# Therapist Control

## The therapist stays in charge

- Goals and targets
- Exercise selection
- Interpretation of progress
- Clinical next steps

Wulo handles delivery, structure, and visibility. The therapist remains the decision-maker.

---

# What Gets Easier

## Workflow value for therapists

- Less homework chasing
- Faster review before sessions
- Clearer parent follow-through
- More continuity between appointments

Speaker note:
The value is not just that children practice. It is that therapists return to better context.

---

# How It Works

## A simple therapist-led care loop

1. Therapist assigns practice matched to the child's goals
2. Child practices between sessions at home or school
3. Parent supports with clearer structure
4. Therapist reviews what happened before the next appointment

# Appendix: Objection Handling

## Common questions

**Does Wulo replace therapy?**  
No. It supports therapist-led practice and review between sessions.

**Will this create more admin?**  
The goal is the opposite: less chasing, faster review, and clearer context.

**Can families use it without therapist involvement?**  
Wulo is designed for therapist-supervised use, with clear boundaries around care and interpretation.
