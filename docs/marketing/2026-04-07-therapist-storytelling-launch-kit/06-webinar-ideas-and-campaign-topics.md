# Webinar Ideas and Campaign Topics

**Purpose:** Create trust-rich, founder-led events that generate therapist conversations and support pilot outreach.

## Webinar Strategy

The webinar should not feel like a product demo disguised as education. It should teach a real therapist problem first, then show where Wulo fits.

The most effective structure is:

1. Name the workflow problem
2. Teach the market something useful
3. Show a practical framework
4. Demonstrate where Wulo helps
5. Invite therapists into a pilot or feedback loop

## Recommended 30-Minute Webinar Format

### Minutes 0-5

Set up the problem:

- what breaks down between sessions
- why families struggle to follow through
- why therapists lose visibility

### Minutes 5-15

Teach the framework:

- therapist-set targets
- child-friendly guided practice
- parent support with less guessing
- structured therapist review

### Minutes 15-22

Show where Wulo fits:

- short workflow demo
- one concrete use case
- clear boundary statement

### Minutes 22-27

Live Q and A

### Minutes 27-30

Pilot invitation and next steps

## Campaign Topics Around the Webinar
