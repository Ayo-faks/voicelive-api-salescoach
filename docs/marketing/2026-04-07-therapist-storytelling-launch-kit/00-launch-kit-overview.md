# Wulo Therapist Storytelling Launch Kit

**Date:** April 7, 2026  
**Primary audience:** Independent paediatric SLTs and small clinics  
**Secondary audience:** SEND schools, SENCOs, and school-linked SLTs

## Purpose

This launch kit turns Wulo's current positioning into a therapist-first GTM package that can support pilot outreach, landing-page refinement, founder-led selling, and content publishing.

## Recommended brand line

**Payoff line:** So your care keeps working, even when the session ends.

Why it works:

- It is therapist-centered, not AI-centered.
- It reinforces continuity of care rather than novelty.
- It fits Wulo's core product story: guided between-session practice, parent follow-through, and therapist review.

Recommended usage:

- Use it as a supporting line under the hero headline, not as the headline itself.
- Pair it with a clearer primary promise such as: `The practice layer for care between sessions.`

## Strategic recommendation

For the next 30 days, keep the message narrow:

- Lead with therapists and clinics.
- Treat schools as a supporting proof path, not the main homepage buyer.
- Position Wulo as therapist-supervised structured practice between sessions.
- Avoid broad AI language and avoid sounding like a consumer speech app.

## Core message architecture

**Hero promise:** Wulo helps therapists extend care beyond the session.

**Proof themes:**

- Children get structured guided practice.
- Parents get a clear role at home.
- Therapists review faster with more context.
- Care continuity improves between appointments.

**Boundary statement:**

Wulo does not replace diagnosis, assessment, or therapist judgment. It supports therapist-led practice and review.
