## Where Wulo Wins

### vs Mable Therapy
Mable delivers **live therapy sessions** via remote SLTs. It scales linearly — more children means more therapist hours. Wulo scales the **practice layer** without adding therapist hours. The two are complementary, not directly substitutive. Wulo wins on:
- Between-session practice (Mable has no structured home practice tool)
- AI-powered pronunciation feedback without a live therapist
- Lower unit cost per child interaction
- Therapist efficiency (review dashboard vs. live session delivery)

**Risk:** Mable could build a practice layer. Move fast on therapist adoption and data.

### vs SuperPenguin
SuperPenguin empowers **caregivers** with guidance and self-directed plans. It targets "waiting well" — supporting families while they wait for live therapy. Wulo wins on:
- Real-time AI voice interaction (SuperPenguin is text/video guidance)
- Pronunciation scoring and measurable speech outcomes
- Directly therapist-controlled exercise authoring
- Broader scope (SuperPenguin currently covers stammering, speech sounds, language delay)

**Overlap:** Both target waiting-list support. Differentiate on real-time AI practice vs. caregiver education.

### vs BetterSpeech
BetterSpeech is a **US teletherapy marketplace** — parents book live video sessions with licensed SLPs. No UK presence, insurance-driven model. Wulo wins on:
- UK market presence and GDPR/safeguarding compliance
- AI-powered practice between sessions (BetterSpeech is session-only)
- Lower cost (no per-session therapist fee for practice)
- Therapist workflow integration (not a marketplace)

**Non-threat in UK short term.** Watch if they expand internationally.

### vs Speech Blubs
Speech Blubs is the **dominant consumer app** — 10M+ downloads, video modelling, face filters, gamification. But it has no therapist oversight, no clinical workflow, and no UK-specific focus. Wulo wins on:
- Therapist-directed exercises (not generic content)
- Pronunciation scoring and structured review
- Clinical credibility and safeguarding posture
- School and LA sales channel (Speech Blubs is pure D2C)

**Risk:** Parents may compare Wulo to Speech Blubs on UX polish and fun factor. Invest in child-friendly design.

### vs Speech Therapy App UK
A simple, low-cost UK articulation app with flashcards, memory games, and spoken exercises. Designed for home practice with SLT guidance. Wulo wins on:
- Real-time AI voice buddy (not static drills)
- Pronunciation feedback and scoring
- Therapist dashboard and review workflow
- Scalable school/LA deployment

**Low threat.** Different product category. Useful price anchor (£24.99 one-time vs. Wulo subscription).

---

## Key Takeaway

Wulo occupies a **unique position**: AI-powered, real-time speech practice that is therapist-controlled and scalable. No current UK competitor combines all three of: (1) real-time AI voice interaction, (2) therapist-directed exercise authoring and review, and (3) pronunciation scoring.

The biggest competitive risk is **Mable Therapy** building a practice layer, or a well-funded consumer app (Speech Blubs) adding a therapist workflow. Speed to therapist adoption and measurable outcomes data is the primary defence.
