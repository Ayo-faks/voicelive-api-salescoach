# Speech Therapy Practice Support Platform

## Challenge Simulation Document

### Purpose

Use this prompt to simulate the likely challenges a team may face when turning a speech therapy practice support platform from a prototype or research idea into a real commercial product.

### Scenario

Imagine your team is building a speech therapy practice support platform that helps children practice speaking through guided exercises, real-time feedback, progress tracking, and therapist review tools.

Your task is to identify important challenges the team will likely face as the platform moves toward launch and long-term use.

## Challenge Areas

### 1. Machine-Learning Challenge

Identify one machine-learning challenge the team may face.

Examples:

- Making speech scoring fair across different accents, ages, and speech patterns
- Avoiding incorrect feedback when audio quality is poor
- Handling variation in child speech without overcorrecting

### 2. Engineering Challenge

Identify one engineering challenge in building the product.

Examples:

- Supporting reliable real-time audio and transcription
- Connecting speech analysis, exercises, and therapist dashboards
- Keeping the product fast and stable across devices

### 3. Operations and Update Challenge

Identify one challenge in operating and updating the product after launch.

Examples:

- Monitoring system quality over time
- Updating models without disrupting therapy sessions
- Fixing bugs safely while keeping the service available

### 4. Team or Management Challenge

Identify one team or management challenge.

Examples:

- Coordinating therapists, designers, engineers, and AI specialists
- Balancing speed of delivery with quality and safety
- Managing changing priorities during product development

### 5. Business Challenge

Identify one business challenge.

Examples:

- Proving value to clinics, schools, or families
- Choosing a pricing model that is sustainable
- Building trust with buyers and decision-makers

### 6. Safety or Ethics Challenge

Identify one safety or ethics challenge.

Examples:

- Protecting child data and session privacy
- Preventing harmful, misleading, or discouraging feedback
- Ensuring the platform supports therapists rather than replacing clinical judgment

## Discussion Prompt

As a group, discuss one challenge from each category:

1. One machine-learning challenge
2. One engineering challenge
3. One operations and update challenge
4. One team or management challenge
5. One business challenge
6. One safety or ethics challenge

## Short Response Format

Use the following sentence starters if needed:

- One challenge is...
- A hard part could be...
- The team may struggle with...
- One risk is...
- A safe approach would be...

## Example Response

One machine-learning challenge is making speech feedback accurate for children with different accents and speech needs.

One engineering challenge is building reliable real-time audio tools that work well during live practice.

One operations challenge is updating the system without interrupting therapist or child sessions.

One team challenge is getting therapists and engineers aligned on what good feedback should sound like.

One business challenge is convincing clinics that the platform saves time and improves outcomes.

One safety or ethics challenge is protecting child voice data and making sure feedback is always supportive.

## Optional Reflection

After discussing the six challenge areas, reflect on these questions:

1. Which challenge seems hardest to solve?
2. Which challenge should be addressed first?
3. Which challenge could most affect children and therapists if handled poorly?
