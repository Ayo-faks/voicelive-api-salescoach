# Wulo Webinar Funnel

## Funnel Goal

Turn warm therapist interest into webinar registrations, post-event trust, and therapist demo or pilot conversations.

## Primary Audience

- Independent paediatric SLTs
- Small clinic owners
- School-linked therapists who care about between-session continuity

## Core Webinar Positioning

The webinar should teach a real workflow problem first, then show where Wulo fits.

It should not feel like a product demo disguised as education.

## Recommended Webinar Title

**Extending Care Beyond The Session: A Better Practice Loop For Child Speech Work**

## Alternate Titles

1. `Why Home Practice Breaks Down And How Therapists Can Improve Follow-Through`
2. `Keep Care Working Between Sessions`
3. `Supporting Children Between Speech Therapy Appointments Without Replacing Clinical Judgment`

## Webinar Promise

In 30 minutes, therapists should leave with:

- a clearer language for the between-session problem;
- a practical framework for continuity, review, and follow-through;
- a better understanding of where digital support helps and where therapist judgment remains central.
