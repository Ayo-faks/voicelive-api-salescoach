# Wulo Branding and Marketing Pack

Date: April 16, 2026

## Purpose

This folder packages the current Wulo brand and campaign thinking into reusable working documents for founder-led storytelling, therapist outreach, LinkedIn publishing, webinar promotion, and landing-page refinement.

The strategic center of gravity is consistent across all files:

- Wulo is therapist-supervised, not therapist-replacing.
- The strongest commercial story is therapist-led continuity between sessions.
- Access and affordability should be framed as better reach, better continuity, and less dead time, not as "cheap therapy."

## Files In This Folder

1. `01-brand-platform-best-practice.md`
   Distills the campaign conversation into a structured best-practice brand platform, including message architecture, headline options, and audience positioning.

2. `02-30-day-linkedin-campaign.md`
   A full 30-day LinkedIn campaign with daily themes, creative angles, suggested formats, CTAs, and post copy.

3. `03-webinar-funnel.md`
   A webinar funnel for therapist acquisition, including invite email copy, registration page copy, reminder emails, and post-event follow-up sequences.

4. `04-anchor-posts.md`
   Three cornerstone founder-voice LinkedIn posts — leverage, continuity, and mission — that every other campaign asset orbits.

5. `05-30-day-posts-polished.md`
   Publish-ready founder-voice copy for all 30 days of the LinkedIn campaign, aligned to the weekly value ladder.

## Recommended Usage Order

1. Start with `01-brand-platform-best-practice.md` to align the message and internalise the therapist value ladder.
2. Publish the three posts in `04-anchor-posts.md` as pinned cornerstone content.
3. Use `02-30-day-linkedin-campaign.md` and `05-30-day-posts-polished.md` to drive steady top-of-funnel attention and demo interest.
4. Use `03-webinar-funnel.md` to convert warm interest into conversations, demos, and pilot applications.

## Operating Rule

If a message makes the therapist feel disintermediated, it is off-strategy.

If a message makes the therapist feel more effective, more trusted, and more able to reach children who would otherwise lose momentum, it is on-strategy.
